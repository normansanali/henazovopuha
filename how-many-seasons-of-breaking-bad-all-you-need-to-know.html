How Many Seasons Of Breaking Bad: All You Need To Know

Is Breaking Bad 5 or 6 seasons?

Breaking Bad has 5 seasons consisting of 62 episodes.

Are there 7 seasons of Breaking Bad?

There isn’t any season 7 of Breaking Bad as of now.

Is season 6 of Breaking Bad the last?

The sixth season of the series premiered on 18th

April 2022.

Why was Breaking Bad only 5 seasons?

The writer cited his experience writing for ‘The X-Files’ and that show’s inability to end properly as the main reasons he choose to only give ‘Breaking Bad’ five seasons.

Why did Walt poison, Lydia?

Lydia was poisoned by Walt in order to protect himself and family as she was the only Madrigal employee still working on the meth operation and was a threat to Walt’s family.

Why does Breaking Bad end so abruptly?

The show continued for a sixth season, but the decision to end it was likely in the best interests of both the creators and fans.
