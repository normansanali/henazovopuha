Legend Ke Koa Mai Ka Mauna A Ke Kai Galeng | News, Sports, Jobs

Legend K Galeng was 8 months old, Born on April 13th 2018 he’s spent all his life in Kapiolani Medical Center (NICU) & God has taken him on December 18th 2018 from Mom (Jasmine Faria) & Dad(Akela Galeng)’s arms

Legend’s Survivor’s, Mom (Jasmine Faria) Dad (Akela Galeng) Grandma (Jovina Texeira) Grandpa (Jaime Faria) Great Grandma (Stanette Horikawa) Grandma (Misty Gomes) Grandpa (Wayne Galeng Jr) Great Grandma (Carina Comes) Great Grandpa (Wayne Gomes)
