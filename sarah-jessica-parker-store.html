Sarah Jessica Parker to Open SJP Collection Store in Washington D.C.

From collaborations with Net-A-Porter to Instagramming her shoes all over NYC, Sarah Jessica Parker is showing no signs of slowing down her growing shoe empire. Today, the actress-turned-designer announced the latest feat for SJP Collection: its first standalone store.

Perhaps most surprising about the new store, however, is the fact that it won't be located in New York City—an obvious choice for both Parker and her alter ego, Carrie Bradshaw—but instead in Washington D.C. The actress took to Instagram to reveal the first store's location: MGM National Harbor, a new casino and luxury retail center located in the heart of D.C.

Set to open on December 8th, the SJP Collection store will carry the brand's line of shoes (which previously were only available at Bloomingdale's and Nordstrom) along with its recently launched line of little black dresses and jewelry.

Looks like it's time to make a trip to DC...
