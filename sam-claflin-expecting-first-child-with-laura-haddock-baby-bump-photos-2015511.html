Sam Claflin Expecting First Child With Laura Haddock: Baby Bump Photos

Fatherhood for Finnick! Sam Claflin and his actress wife, Laura Haddock, are expecting their first child together. The mom-to-be debuted her baby bump at The Hunger Games: Mockingjay – Part 2 premiere in London on Thursday, Nov. 5.

The Me Before You star, 29, sweetly caressed his wife’s growing belly on the red carpet. He looked dapper in a black suit while Haddock, 30, wore a tight-fitting bedazzled dress. She rocked a bold red lip and kept her locks in a low bun.

The British couple — who married in July 2013 after two years of dating — appeared to be over the moon about their next chapter. Claflin’s parents are ecstatic to be grandparents, too.
