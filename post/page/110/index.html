Aspheric Eyeglass Lenses - All About Vision

Aug 6, 2024 By Gary Heiting, OD With aspheric eyeglass lenses, thin is in. Advanced optical design technology allows aspheric eyeglass lenses to be made with flatter curves than conventional lenses, giving them a slimmer, more attractive profile. Conventional lenses have a front surface that is spherical, meaning it has the same curve across its entire surface, much like a baseball. Aspheric lenses, on the other hand, have a more complex front surface that gradually changes in curvature from the center of the lens out to the edge.
