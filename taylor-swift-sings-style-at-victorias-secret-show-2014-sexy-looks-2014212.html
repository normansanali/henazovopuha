Taylor Swift Sings &quot;Style&quot; at Victoria's Secret Show 2014: Sexy Looks

Style for days! Taylor Swift performed her strut-inducing song “Style” off her mega-smash album 1989 at the 2014 Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show in London on Tuesday, Dec. 2 — and didn’t leave much to the imagination!

The 24-year-old, like the Angels beside her, had a few dress changes during her second consecutive year on the runway. Some of her outfits included a cleavage-baring slip with a cream-colored floor-length robe over a satin negligee. Later, while singing the Harry Styles-inspired tune “Style,” she bared even more in sheer black lace lingerie.

Hours before the big show, Swift spent time with several of the VS models, including BFFs Karlie Kloss and Lily Aldridge. She also teased what songs she would be belting out.
