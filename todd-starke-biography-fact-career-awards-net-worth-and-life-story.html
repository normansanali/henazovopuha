Todd Thomas Starke was an American actor. He was known for his roles in The Doris Day Show (1968), Adam-12 (1968) and Angel in My Pocket (1969). 

Starke was born on December 19, 1961 in Los Angeles, California. 

In the years 1968-1971, he played the character of Toby Martin in The Doris Day Show. 

In 1969, he appeared in the Alan Rafkin directed comedy film Angel in My Pocket. In 1971, he appeared as Danny in  television series Adam-12. 

Death

He died on May 4, 1983 in a motorcycle accident in Los Angeles, California.
