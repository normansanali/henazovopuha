Matt Leinart News - Us Weekly

Name:Matt Leinart

Hometown:Santa Ana, CA

Birthday:May 11, 1983

About

The towering 6′ 5″ Arizona Cardinals quarterback was born on May 11, 1983 in Santa Ana, Calif.

After winning the Heisman Trophy and the Walter Camp Award as college football player of the year while playing for the University of Southern California Trojans, Leinart joined the Arizona Cardinals in 2006 as their quarterback.

Off the field, he pals around with Nick Lachey and has been romantically linked to Paris Hilton, Laguna Beach’s Kristin Cavallari and former assistant to Jessica Simpson, Cacee Cobb.

In 2006, Leinart had a son, Cole, with Brynn Cameron, an ex-girlfriend he had met during study hall at USC.
