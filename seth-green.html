Seth Green Biography

Seth Green is best known as Actor, comedian, voice actor, television producer, television director and screenwriter who has an estimated Net Worth of $40 Million. Comedic actor who became known as Oz on Buffy the Vampire Slayer and won multiple Emmy Awards for creating and producing the television show Robot Chicken. His film credits include The Italian Job, Rat Race and Without a Paddle.  He was born on February 8,1974, Philadelphia, PA. He played Dr. Evil's son Scott in the Austin Powers films.  One of his first film roles was 1984's The Hotel New Hampshire, which starred Jodie Foster and Rob Lowe. He starred alongside Mike Myers in the Austin Powers films. His parents are Herb Green and Barbara Gesshel, and he has a sister named Kaela. He married actress and co-star Clare Grant in May 2010.  His zodiac sign is Aquarius.
