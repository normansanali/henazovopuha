Meet Alexandra Jarvis! Bio, Net Worth, Height, Age

One of the three Alexandra on Netflix’s Selling The OC is Alexandra Jarvis. Fans are interested to learn more about Alexandra’s net worth career, ex-husband, age, and height.

Learn all details here in this article because we got it all covered here.

Alexandra Jarvis On Netflix’s Selling The OC

Netflix premiered “Selling the OC” on 24 August 2022, a spin-off series Selling Sunsets. The show follows eleven brand new real estate agents who sell luxury waterfront properties in Orange County, California. Stars of “Selling Sunset” According to the Real Deal, the Oppenheim Group, a brokerage owned by Jason and Brett Oppenheim, will expand to Newport Beach in 2021. A completely new group of agents—and cast members—came with them.

Moreover, Alex is one of the 11 agents appearing on the show.

Speaking with Today, Alexandra shared, “Rose and I are the top producing female agents in the office”. They labeled each other as “unstoppable”. Whereas, three words describing her this season are “Confident, tenacious, and irreverent”. Two Alexandras met each other in a professional setting but they became good friends.

In a recent exclusive interview with ET, Rose and Jarvis essentially revealed their interpersonal bond, as well as their group standing, is the same as it was during filming. “I don’t talk to [most of the realtors at the brokerage],” Rose said. “I just address the issues and address the rumors that they’re stirring. And they get offended because I’m confronting them about what they’re talking about and their gossip… They think I’m just stirring the pot and starting drama. It’s just such bulls**t.”

Jarvis added to that statement, “I would second that because it’s like if they’re trying to say [Rose is] the pot stirrer, I mean, it’s like Alex Hall made the pot of soup and then gave her the spoon.”

Others joining her in this 8-episode journey are Alex Hall, Alexandra Rose, Austin Victoria, Brandi Marshall, Gio Helou, Kayla Cardona, Lauren Brito, Polly Brindle, Sean Palmieri, and Tyler Stanaland.

Alexandra Jarvis Net Worth

In 2022, Alexandra Jarvis should have a net worth of $4 million, according to The Cinemaholic. It’s important to know that the majority of top-performing real estate agents in California earn a set yearly income of roughly $150,000. Her estimated annual income is around $500K.

Alexandra was interested in finances and business from the start. She did not decide to pursue a career in real estate. So, as soon as she graduated from high school, she enrolled at Auburn University to pursue a degree in Spanish and international business.

Though she immediately changed her mind, Alexandra went on to earn her law degree from the UC Irvine School of Law and joined a firm in Newport Beach where she practiced commercial litigation and employment law.

It was interesting to hear Alexandra say that while working in the legal area, she improved her client management abilities as well as her negotiation and problem-solving skills. She was able to stand out once she made the decision to change careers and work in real estate thanks to these abilities and her bubbly personality.

On her Oppenheim Group bio, Alexandra has been described as a result-oriented businesswoman who positioned herself at the top of the Orange County real estate market, closing nearly $40 million in sales during her first year in the industry. All thanks to her expertise, understanding, and legal background.

According to Zillow, in the last 12 months, she has made 1 sale in San Juan Capistrano with a closing price of $8,675,000.

Who Are Alexandra Jarvis Parents?

Talking about her parents, Alexandra Jarvis is the daughter of Robert J. Jarvis Jr. and Pamela Jarvis. She lost her dad in early September 2015. He was only 58 years old. On Father’s Day 2020, she remembered her dad via an IG post. In the post, she wrote he taught her values that would serve her entire life.

In one section, Alexandra wrote, “He taught me from a young age how to succeed in this world as a confident, independent woman – paying careful attention not to undermine me because of my femininity, but teaching me to embrace it as an asset.”

Alexandra remembered him as a “rare man of integrity that is hardly found in modern culture”.

As for her mother, Pamela turned 65 years old in May 2022. She graduated from Baton Rouge Fine Arts Academy. She is originally from Irving, Texas currently resides in Aliso Viejo, California.

Alexandra is close to her mom and dedicated a Mother’s Day post to Pamela in May 2022. She called her ‘the most strong’ yet ‘kind woman’ she had ever met. She added that she inherited her love for chocolate from Pamela.

More about her family, Alexandra has a brother named Robb Jarvis. Born in August 1988, Robert is 34 years old.

Alexandra Jarvis grew up in a close-knit family that taught her the value of having a loving and peaceful home and instilled in her family values. It’s interesting to note that Alexandra attributes her success as a real estate agent to her background and emphasized how her desire to assist her clients in finding their ideal home motivates her.
