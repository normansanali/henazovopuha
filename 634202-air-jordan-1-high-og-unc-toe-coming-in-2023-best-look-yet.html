Air Jordan 1 High OG &quot;UNC Toe&quot; Coming In 2023: Best Look Yet

We are only in the early stages of 2023, which means there is still plenty of time for High OG colorways to hit the market. As it turns out, many of the models that fans are hoping for will be delivered in the summer and the Holidays. However, we are still being met with all kinds of teasers for these upcoming releases. This is great for fans as it means they will have a much longer time to decide whether or not they fancy a particular model.

One of the latest Air Jordan 1 High OGs to be shown off is this "UNC Toe" colorway. Thanks to @sneakertigger and @zsneakerheadz, we now have an in-hand look at the shoe. Initially, the sneaker was simply known through photoshop renderings. However, we now have actual pictures of the sneaker. Overall, it looks amazing as powder blue mixes with black and white. This is simply one of those timeless looks that fans will always enjoy. Simply put, it is very difficult to hate something that looks like this.

As for the actual release date, it has been rumored that these will drop on July 8th. However, this release date has not yet been confirmed. There is still a lot of time before these get released, so expect some changes along the way. Until then, be sure to stay tuned to HNHH for the latest news and updates from around the sneaker world.
