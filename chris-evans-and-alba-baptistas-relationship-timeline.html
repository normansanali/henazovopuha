Chris Evans and Alba Baptistas Relationship Timeline

Captain America found his girl. Chris Evans and reported wife Alba Baptista were first spotted holding hands in November 2022, one year after the pair initially sparked romance rumors.

“I’m gonna give you a good answer,” the Gray Man star said in an interview with Shonda Rhimes’ Shondaland in July 2022, when asked about what he was focused on. “The answer would be that maybe [I’m] laser-focused on finding a partner, you know, someone that you want to live with.”

In addition to their public outings, Evans publicly commented on the Warrior Nun actress’ Instagram upload about her film Mrs. Harris Goes to Paris premiering in her home country of Portugal. Evans commented on the upload with a clapping hands emoji and a “melting” smiley face. Then, he shared a PDA-heavy social media tribute to her in January 2023 that made it clear they were serious.

Prior to his relationship with Baptista, the Boston native famously was linked to high-profile stars such as Jessica Biel and Minka Kelly. He dated Jenny Slate after costarring with the comedian in Gifted, but the two called it quits for good in March 2018.
