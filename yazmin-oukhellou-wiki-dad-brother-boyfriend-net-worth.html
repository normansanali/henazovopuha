Who Is Yazmin Oukhellou Dad? + Her Wiki Brother, Boyfriend, Net Worth

In 2021, The Only Way Is Essex star Yazmin Oukhellou was in the limelight as she lost her grandmother Hilda on Sunday. Yazmin paid an emotional tribute to her nanny, who died of respiratory failure in Barnet hospital, through an Instagram post.

Love you forever Nan,” to break the tragic news.

Since Yazmin is already on the news, we will talk about her boyfriend, age, career, and more in this wiki article.

How Old Is Yazmin Oukhellou?

Yazmin Oukhellou was born on May 3, 1994, and turned 27 years old in 2021. She celebrated her 27th birthday on May 3, 2021. Yazmin’s zodiac sign is Taurus.

Who Is Yazmin Oukhellou Dad?

Hamid Oukhellou is Yazmin Oukhellou’s dad. He appeared in three episodes of The Only Way Is Essex as Yaz’s father.

As for his jobs, Hamid has been serving as a director at Grays Tyres and UK Garage Limited since February 2000. He is also a director at UK Properties Services.

As of October 2021, Adam runs his own garage business.

Yazmin Oukhellou Boyfriend

For a long time, Yazmin was in an on/off relationship with her boyfriend, James Lock. Yazmin joined the ITVBe show in 2017 alongside James.

Reportedly, Yazmin and her ex Jake McLean have settled their differences to date once again. Both of them are in Dubai. While many people had no faith in the revival of their relationship, the pictures from Jake McLean’s birthday have proved everyone wrong.

“Yazmin and Jake rekindled their relationship when they were both single again… When Yazmin split with James, Jake got back in touch and they started talking again, ” a source said.
