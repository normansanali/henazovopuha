Richard Roundtree Wife: Was Richard Roundtree Married?

Richard Roundtree was married twice in his life. His first wife was Mary Jane Grant, whom he married on November 27, 1963.

Roundtree and Grant had two children, John and Nicole, before divorcing in December 1973.

Roundtree later married Karen M. Ciernia in September 1980. Together they had three children, Morgan, Taylor, and Evan. Roundtree and Ciernia divorced in 1998.

Roundtree was a very private person and rarely spoke about his personal life in public.

However, he did say in an interview in 2013 that he was grateful for his family and that they were the most important thing in his life.

Here is a summary of Richard Roundtree’s wives:

Mary Jane Grant (married 1963-1973, 2 children)
Karen M. Ciernia (married 1980-1998, 3 children)

Roundtree died on October 24, 2023, at the age of 81, after a brief battle with pancreatic cancer. He is survived by his five children and his grandchildren.
