How much is Janet Jacksons Net Worth in 2023

Janet Jackson is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and dancer who has an estimated net worth of US$360 million as of June 2023. She is one of the wealthiest musicians in the United States. One of America’s favorite musician of all time.

A successful career in both the music and movie industries are her main sources of income. Jackson has also made substantial amounts of money from investments, endorsements and other business ventures. Royalties from Spotify, Amazon Music, YouTube, and Apple Music also add up to her total net worth.

Who is Janet Jackson?

Janet Damita Jo Jackson known simply as Janet Jackson was born on May 16, 1966 in Gary, Indiana in the United States. She is an American and currently 57 years of age in 2023. She is the youngest among the ten children of her parents Joe Jackson and Katherine Jackson. She is the younger sister of late pop singer Michael Jackson.

The Jackson family relocated to Los Angeles’ Encino neighborhood after the Jackson 5 signed a record deal with Motown in March 1969. She and her brothers debuted on the variety show “The Jacksons” in 1976, launching their careers in show business.

Her father and manager Joseph Jackson obtained a recording contract for her with A&M Records after she appeared in various projects, including starring roles in the sitcoms “Good Times” (1977) and “A New Kind of Family” (1979-1980).
