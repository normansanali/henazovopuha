Jewell Loyd Parents: Meet Dad Calvin Loyd &amp; Mom Gwendolyn Davis-Loyd

Basketball player, Jewell Loyd’s parents Calvin Loyd & Gwendolyn Davis-Loyd are proud of their daughter’s achievements in the Olympics and WNBA. Let’s get to know Loyd’s parents better!

Jewell Loyd is an American professional basketball player for Perfumerias Avenida of Spain’s Liga Femenina de Baloncesto and Seattle Storm of the Women’s National Basketball Association.

She is a well-known player and was drafted in the 2015 WNBA draft by the Seattle Storm.

In her career, she has been in the WBNA All-Star games twice and was also named WNBA Rookie of the Year in 2015.

Jewell Loyd Parents: Calvin Loyd & Gwendolyn Davis-Loyd

Jewell Loyd was born to her parents: father Calvin Loyd & mother Gwendolyn Davis-Loyd, in Lincolnwood, Illinois.

Jewell Loyd comes from an athletic family. Her father, Calvin, is a tennis instructor. He was also her first coach.

She used to play tennis as a youngster where she also won the tournament at the age of 10 in 2004. But, she immediately shifted her interest to basketball after watching the William sisters play who is her role model.

Jewell’s mother Gwendolyn is a retired educator. She recently authored a book “The Invisible Disability” inspired by her daughter which addressed Jewell’s battle with dyslexia.

Her mother appears to be a supportive one as she was found yelling with excitement when Jewell made it to the Olympics.

Moreover, her younger brother Jarryd also played basketball at Valparaiso University.
