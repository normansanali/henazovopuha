Lanny McDonald- Biography

Lanny McDonald was born in Hanna, Canada on February 16, 1953. NHL Hall of Fame center who scored over 500 career goals and tallied over 1,000 points. He spent the bulk of his career with the Toronto Maple Leafs and Calgary Flames. He spent much of the 1982-83 season battling with Wayne Gretzky for the title of the league’s top goal-scorer. He had three straight 40+ goal seasons in his first three years in the NHL.
