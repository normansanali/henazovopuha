Clefable  
ピクシー Pixy
For more information on this Pokémon's species, see Clefable.

Clefable (Japanese: ピクシー Pixy) is a Colorless-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the Expedition Base Set.

Card text

Moonlight
つきのひかり
 
Once during your turn (before your attack), you may put a card from your hand back on your deck. If you do, search your deck for a basic Energy card, show it to your opponent, and put it into your hand. Shuffle your deck afterward. This power can't be used if Clefable is affected by a Special Condition.
Doubleslap
おうふくビンタ		20×
Flip 2 coins. This attack does 20 damage times the number of heads.

e-Reader data

The card ID of the Holofoil print is B-91. The card ID of the Non Holofoil print is B-91-#. On the Non Holofoil print, the short strip contains Pokédex information, a brief card summary, and information on the content of the long strip. The species, height, and weight are present on the Holofoil print.

Animation

The long strip contains data for the Metronome animation. Clefairy from Expedition Base Set is also required to play it.

Release information

This card was included as a Holofoil and Non Holofoil card in the Expedition Base Set, first released in the Japanese Base Expansion Pack. The Non Holo print was one of seven test cards made by Wizards of the Coast before the expansion's release. These cards all have "For Position Only" in white printed on the illustration, "Medium" printed beneath the e-Reader symbol, a long e-Reader data strip (this is the only card to retain the strip upon release), no "ID:" before the card ID, and a Wizards copyright instead of Nintendo. On this print, "PokéPower:" is printed before the Poké-Power's name.

Trivia

Origin

Moonlight and DoubleSlap are both moves in the Pokémon games that Clefable can learn. This card's e-Reader Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Crystal.
