Lisa Rinna Reveals Her Dad Has Died, Read Her Tribute

RIP. Lisa Rinna has revealed that her father, Frank, has passed away by sharing a heartfelt tribute with fans via Instagram.

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star posted a throwback photograph of her late dad with her mother, Lois, on Thursday, January 21.

"The World lost a Great Man today," Rinna captioned the sweet photo. "Heaven got a great Angel. I love you Dad."

The reality TV star, 52, also shared an image of one of her father's paintings. And before announcing her sad news, the onscreen personality posted a picture of her dad and herself on her wedding day.
