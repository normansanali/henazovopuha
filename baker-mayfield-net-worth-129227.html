Baker Mayfield Net Worth

Baker Mayfield was born on April 14, 1995 in Austin, TX and is a 2017 Heisman Trophy winner for the University of Oklahoma. He was also a finalist for the award the previous year and won several other accolades in 2017 including AP Player of the Year. Mayfield helped lead his Sooners to the semifinals of the college football playoff in 2015-16 and 2017-18.

About

2017 Heisman Trophy winner for the University of Oklahoma who was also a finalist for the award the previous year. He won several other accolades in 2017 including AP Player of the Year. He helped lead his Sooners to the semifinals of the college football playoff in 2015-16 and 2017-18. 

Before Fame

He was a walk-on with the Texas Tech Red Raiders before transferring to Oklahoma following alleged issues with scholarship and poor communication with coaches.

Trivia

He became the first walk-on true freshman to start a season opener for a BCS school.

Family Life

He was born in Austin, Texas to parents James and Gina Mayfield. He has been in a relationship with Emily Wilkinson.

Associated With

He was coached by Bob Stoops while at Oklahoma.
