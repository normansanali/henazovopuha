See Goldie Hawn Without Makeup!

Fresh-faced Goldie!

Earlier this week, Kate Hudson's mother Goldie Hawn ditched her normal painted face to go makeup free while running errands in Los Angeles.

Clad in a black tank top and leggings with her hair worn down and loose, Hawn, 66, went au naturel as she toured the site where she is said to be building a new home.

A mother of three and a grandmother of four — including Kate's sons Ryder, 7, and Bingham, 17 months — the legendary actress says her secret to aging gracefully is living in the moment.

"There's a lot of dark, but you have to balance it out," the Overboard and Shampoo star told Us Weekly in 2011. "I'm a real optimist. Have the intention to enjoy life and to have a good time. I love being alive. I cherish every moment."
