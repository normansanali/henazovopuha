Hailey Bieber Pairs an Oversized Leather Blazer with a Tiny Skirt for Date Night with Justin

Hailey Bieber is doing date night in style.

Earlier this week, the model stepped out in New York City with husband Justin Bieber for a low-key dinner at celeb hot spot Carbone. For the occasion, Hailey wore an edgy all-black outfit composed of an oversized leather blazer layered over yet another leather biker jacket, plus a gray sweater with a white collar peeking underneath. She ditched pants and, instead, opted for a tiny black miniskirt.

The model upped the ante with her shoes and accessories, wearing shiny black wedge pumps, rectangular shades with a cobalt blue tint, and a light blue mini bag from Bottega Veneta.

The "Peaches" singer also showed off his own style during the outing. Justin wore a light pink collared chore jacket, a white T-shirt, and black trousers with white polka dots. To top off the look, he added a black beanie and black boots with caramel brown soles.

The Image Direct

Lately, Hailey has had a string of stylish run-ins with the paparazzi. This past weekend, for instance, the Rhode founder ventured out on a coffee run with Justin in New York City, where she embraced the preppy Y2K aesthetic via a long black belted coat from Saint Laurent, a gray pleated skirt from Misbhv, a beige collared knit sweater from Source Unknown, tights by Calzedonia, and chunky leather loafers from Proenza Schouler. She accessorized with black shades and a cream shoulder bag from Bottega Veneta.

Gotham//Getty Images

Hailey opened up about her philosophy for dressing up for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story.

"I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself," she said. "Even if I'm just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!"
