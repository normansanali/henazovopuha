Biography

Riley Smith was born in Cedar Rapids, IA on April 12, 1978. Model and actor who starred in the Disney Channel’s Motocross. He has also appeared in Not Another Teen Movie, National Lampoon’s Barely Legal, and Bring It On. He later starred in The CW’s Frequency, followed by FOX’s Proven Innocent as Levi. He guest-starred in Ghost Whisperer, starring Jennifer Love Hewitt. He began competing in horse shows at age 9 and won a national championship at age 16.
