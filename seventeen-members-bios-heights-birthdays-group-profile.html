Seventeen Members; Bios, Heights, Birthdays, Group Profile

Seventeen is a South Korean entertainment boy group consisting of 13 members under PLEDIS Entertainment, part of HYPE Ladies. The thirteen members of the groups are S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, Vernon (hip hop sub-unit), Woozi, Jeonghan, Joshua, DK, Seungkwan (vocal sub-unit), Hoshi, Jun, The8, and Dino (performance sub-unit). They made their debut on 26th May, 2015 with their hit mini-album titled 17 Carat.
