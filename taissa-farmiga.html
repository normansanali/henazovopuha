Ever since she made her debut in her sister Vera Farmiga’s directorial debut, Taissa Farmiga has earned a reputation for being a horror drama specialist. She has been cast in various important roles in the popular horror TV series franchise American Horror Story. She was also cast in the lead role in the 2018 horror movie, The Nun. However, that doesn’t mean her skills are lacking in other genres. Her work in Ethan Hawke and John Travolta starrer western movie, In a Valley of Violence, has been highly praised.

    Taissa Farmiga Facts

  • Initially, she had no intentions of becoming an actress and instead, wanted to become an accountant. She was looking for universities when she was cast in Higher Ground. 
  • She was persuaded to appear in Higher Ground by her sister Vera Farmiga, who had offered Taissa a 2004 Toyota Tacoma pick-up truck if she agreed to work in the movie.
  • The first-ever audition she did was for her role in American Horror Story and she managed to ace it in a single go.
  • In her free time, she loves to play the guitar.
  • She also considers herself an expert in making origami cranes.
  • When she was born, her mother was 45 years old. While her father had crossed the mark of 51 years.
  • Her solid performance in Higher Ground helped her get a representation contract from the prestigious talent agency, ICM Partners.
  • In 2016, she made her stage debut with the Off-Broadway revival of Sam Shepard’s drama Buried Child. She was cast in an important role of Shelly.
