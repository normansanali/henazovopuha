The Cast Of Netflix's One Piece, Ranked By Net Worth

Netflix's One Piece is the live-action adaptation of the long-running Japanese manga series of the same name. The show, which will debut on the streaming platform sometime in 2023, is centered around the adventures of the Straw Hat Pirates as they navigate the earth in search of One Piece, a famed treasure that will see their captain become The King of Pirates.

The series features a diverse cast of seasoned and rising actors, including Emily Rudd, Morgan Davies, Iñaki Godoy, Mackenyu Arata, Vincent Regan, and Craig Fairbrass, among others. While some cast members have long made a name for themselves in acting, most are hitting their first significant roles with their feature on the Netflix series, automatically placing them on different earning pedestals. Here's the net worth of the cast of Netflix's One Piece.

9 Emily Rudd Is Worth Is About $500,000 - 1 Million

Emily Rudd, who plays Nami, is worth an estimated $1 million, though some sources have her at $500,000. The Fear Street star took an early interest in acting, so after completing her high school education, she pursued a college degree in Performing Arts. Before breaking into acting, the 29-year-old kick-started her career on a small front as a model and actress in her hometown.

After appearing in several shorts, Emily's acting career experienced a major leap in 2008, when she landed roles in The Romanoff and Electric Dreams. Since breaking into the movie industry, Emily has been featured in notable small and big-screen projects, including Fear Street Part Two: 1978, Fear Street Part Three: 1666, Hunters, and Moonshot.

8 Steven John Ward Is Worth $200,000

Steven John Ward portrays Dracule Mihawk, a pirate adjudged as the world's greatest swordsman - a role he refers to as the most favorite character he has ever played on film. After coming in 7th place in the 2010 Amstel Class Act competition, the South African actor has not looked back; he has been featured in commercials and films worldwide. Besides acting, Steven's good looks rake in cash for him as he is a model. His esitmated net worth is believed to be around $200,000.

7 Taz Skylar Is Worth $3 Million

Taz Skylar is a walking bag of talent. He is a producer, writer, and actor, and what's more, he has expressed his art in every capacity. At the start of his career, Taz was featured primarily in short films. Over time, he grew from small projects to starring in big and small screen projects, including Villain,Agatha Raisin, The Deal, and The Lazarus Project.

Taz has written and produced Multi-Facial and The Reserves as a producer and writer. Asides from his upcoming role in One Piece, Taz is part of Amazon Prime's action thriller Gassed Up, where he contributed to the script and stars in a main role. His net worth is estimated to be around $3 million.

6 Morgan Davies Is Worth $1.5 Million

The Australian actor started their career as a child star in the 2010 Australian-French film, The Tree. Morgan's career began with loud ovations as they bagged multiple major award nominations for their role in the movie. The actor had a good run following their first stint, but they eventually took a brief hiatus due to their battles with gender dysphoria.

But like several child stars who made amazing comebacks, Morgan's journey that brought them to The End launched them back into the limelight. From their earnings as an actor, Morgan has an accumulated net worth of $1.5 million.

5 Jeff Ward Is Worth $1.5 Million

Jeff Ward, who is portraying Buggy the Clown in the upcoming Netflix series, is worth an estimated $1.5 million. Although the actor started his career on stage in theatrical productions, he developed his craft, landing his first major role as Charles Manson in Manson's Lost Girls.

Since coming into the limelight, Ward has built an impressive profile with his appearance on notable TV projects, including Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., Channel Zero: No-End House, and Brand New Cherry Flavor, among others.

Outside of acting, Ward might be trying his hands at working behind the scenes as he recently wrote and produced a storytelling audio program, Conference Call, on several platforms.

4 Peter Gadiot Is Worth $6 Million

Peter Gadiot plays the role of Shanks, the captain of the Red-Haired pirates in the upcoming series. Although the British actor started his career a little over a decade ago, he has made a mark in the industry, bagging prominent roles in Once Upon a Time in Wonderland and the recently canceled Queen of the South.

Aside from acting, Gadiot spends his time and resources campaigning against human trafficking and slavery and promoting humanitarian organizations with aligning beliefs. His net worth is believed to be around $6 million.

3 Mackenyu Arata Is Worth $5 Million

Born into a family of creatives with an actor father and brother, and an actress half-sister, it is no surprise that Mackenyu Arata didn't fall far from the tree. The 26-year-old actor who plays Roronoa Zoro was born into entertainment and appeared in films and mini-TV series as a growing teenager. But it wasn't until he turned 15 that he was inspired to pursue acting professionally.

One year later. Mackenyu landed a lead role in the 2015 feature film Take a Chance. In the same year, he gained rave popularity in Japan for starring as Eiji Tomari in Kamen Rider Drive: Surprise Future. With the influence he gained from his popularity, Mackenyu promotes high-end fashion brands and has newly opened his clothing and jewelry line. His net worth is estimated to be around $5 million.

2 Craig Fairbrass Is Worth $8 Million

Craig Fairbrass portrays the role of Chef Zeff, the floating restaurant owner and Sanji's father figure. Fairbrass has an accumulated net worth of $8 million, which places him a close second on the list of the cast members with the most dough. With a career that has lasted over four decades, Fairbrass puts the S in success; he has been featured in films, TV, and video games, including Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare, Battlefield V, and Squadron 42, among others.

1 Vincent Regan Is Worth $13 Million

With an estimated $13 million net worth, Vincent Regan takes the cake for being the wealthiest cast member of Netflix's One Piece. The seasoned actor who plays the role of Garp, a Vice Admiral in the Navy, needs no introduction. With over three decades of actively working as an actor, the 57-year-old has cemented himself among industry greats.

Although he is famous for his ancient Greek, action fantasy, and epic film roles in 300, Troy, and Clash of the Titans, the Rescue Me actor has a prolific career on both small and big screen. Asides from dominating the screen, the actor owns a theatrical company under which he wrote, directed, and developed projects.
