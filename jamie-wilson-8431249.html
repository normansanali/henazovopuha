Jamie Wilson - InStyle

Title: Beauty Writer

Education: University of Florida

Location: Brooklyn, NY

Expertise: Makeup , Fragrance, Hair, Skincare

Jamie is a beauty writer and editor with 10 years of editorial experience. She was most recently the beauty editor at Harper’s BAZAAR, working across both print and digital to produce celebrity interviews, cover stories, trend pieces, features, and SEO stories. Now freelance, she writes beauty stories that have appeared in Vogue, WWD, and Glamour, among many others. She loves to test and review skincare, haircare, fragrance, and makeup and share the best of the best with readers.
