Richie Cannata Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Richie Cannata (born March 3, 1949) is an American music producer, multi-instrumentalist and studio owner. He is mainly famous for playing saxophone in the Billy Joel band alongside Doug Stegmeyer and Liberty DeVitto. After quitting the band in 1981, he founded Cove City Sound Studios in Glen Cove, New York. Artists including Celine Dion, Billy Joel, Jennifer Lopez and Marc Anthony have recorded at Cannata’s studio. Cannata was with Tommy Shaw’s band in the mid-80s, appearing on Shaw’s first three solo albums. In the mid-1990s Cannata travelled with The Beach Boys, playing saxophone, woodwinds and synthesisers.
