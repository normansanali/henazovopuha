Cutter Gauthier Age, Brother, College, Instagram, Scouting Report

Cutter Gauthier is 18 years old. He was born on January, 19 2004.

Cutter Gauthier Brother

There have been speculations that Canadian ice hockey forward, Julien Gauthier is Cutter’s brother.

But the two are not related in any way.

Cutter Gauthier College

Cutter attended Boston College of the National Collegiate Athletic Association, where he played ice hockey.

Cutter Gauthier Instagram

Cutter is on Instagram @cuttergauthier_ with 6178 followers.

Cutter Gauthier Scouting Report

Cutter takes tremendous quick steps at first. His edgework and agility are top notch.

He change directions with ease and knows how to maneuver through traffic with and without the puck.

Cutter also exudes strength and stability on his skates, he makes his way through balanced checks and can drive the net with ease. He also knows how to establish position around the net.

Knocking him off the puck is very difficult.

Cutter accurately uses his wrist shot and snapshot to score most of his goals. If he shoots off the wrong foot, finding his balance is not a problem.

However, he needs to improve on his slap shot and one-timer. He also needs to learn how to adjust and get his shot off with much accuracy.
