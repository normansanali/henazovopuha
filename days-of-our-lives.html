Jul 19, 2024 Martina Birk

Days of Our Lives

We have the latest Days of Our Lives spoilers from Monday, July 1, to Friday, July 12. A desperate offer is made, one man has a difficult request, and a secret summer plan is revealed — plus, Nicole can’t believe what she learns about Eric. Read on for that and more heartache ahead in Salem…

Days of Our Lives spoilers week of July 1:

In honor of Canada Day, we bring you photos of soap stars from the ‘Great White North’!

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Monday, July 1:

In today’s recap, EJ offers Melinda a job to keep her quiet, Tate asks Holly to runaway with him, and Eric gets a job offer.

It’s been a long time in coming, but at long last, Stefan and Gabi find themselves sharing a blissful reunion.

Ava can’t stop feeling guilty, but Kristen — who’s not typically one to feel that particular emotion — tries helping her see it’s futile.

Tate’s hoping that if his dad talks to Theresa, perhaps he’ll finally be allowed to openly see Holly. At the same time, Holly is asking Eric to throw his support behind the union.

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Tuesday, July 2:

In today’s recap, Kayla reveals Paulina was not responsible for Chanel’s miscarriage, Kate and Abe get into the soap opera business, and Bobby refuses to let Everett out.

Paulina needs clarity where Chanel is concerned. Will Kayla be able to set the concerned mom’s mind at ease?

Will Abe give Kate’s latest idea his undivided attention… and perhaps even his support?

Actions have consequences, and Steve is about to find out what helping Clyde escape will cost!

Will Stephanie be willing to help Marlena where Everett’s concerned?

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Wednesday, July 3:

In today’s recap, Chad wants to exhume Abigail’s body, and Bobby claims he can help Jada solve a murder.

Following an important talk with Kayla, Chad heads straight to Paulina in order to make what might be a difficult request.

How will Jada react when Stephanie suggests she pay a visit to Bobby?

Fresh off their recent conversation, Kate has to share a bit of bad news with Abe — which could actually be good news for them.

Steve’s fate is finally revealed when he opens up to Marlena.

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Thursday, July 4:

In today’s recap, Xander rejects Victor’s money, Justin forces Theresa to agree to a prenup, and someone spies on Tate and Holly.

Whether Brady has the best or worst of intentions, one thing’s for sure: Theresa is about to completely misinterpret them!

Ah, summer, when young lovers like Tate and Holly cook up schemes which will allow them to be together, no matter what their folks say!

Justin offers Alex some legal advice, but will his wise words be taken to heart?

Maggie does something that is going to knock the kilt, or at least the socks, right off of Xander.

Find out why Days of Our Lives earned a B- grade here. Also, which show did way, way worse!

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Friday, July 5:

In today’s recap, Gabi announces Eric is Jude’s father at EJ’s press conference, and Connie recalls stabbing Li!

Nicole couldn’t possibly have prepared for what Eric tells her: He’s leaving Salem… immediately!

Now that she’s in charge of DiMera Enterprises, Kristen contemplates putting Melinda in a powerful position!

Jada stuns Rafe by filling him in on what Bobby is now claiming. Meanwhile, Everett… or Bobby… or both do their best to prevent Marlena from making progress.

That was fast! Arianne Zucker is back at work already — and wait till you see her wild new look!

Days of Our Lives spoilers week of July 8:

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Monday, July 8:

As we get closer and closer to her portrayer’s exit, Nicole finds out a bit of news about her son that is going to blow her away. Meanwhile, EJ makes it clear that he intends to do as much harm as possible to Stefan and Gabi, whom he wants to see punished!

When new info comes to light about Li Shin’s murder, will we finally learn who was responsible?

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Tuesday, July 9:

Stefan is thrilled to have Gabi back in his life, but that doesn’t mean he’s going to just let everything go. In fact, he wants answers from his lady love!

Get ready, because today’s the day everyone’s been waiting for as Eric finally learns the truth from Nicole.

When EJ opens up to make a confession, how will Holly react to what he has to share?

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Wednesday, July 10:

Chad is desperate to find answers about Abigail, and he’s come to the conclusion that there’s really only one way to uncover the truth. But Jack could wind up standing in the way after hearing what Chad wants to do!

Justin opens up to Xander about the difficult time he’s had and the pain he’s felt since losing Alex as a son.

Maggie thinks she knows how to heal the ever-expanding rift within the family. But will Alex and Theresa go along with the plan she proposes?

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Thursday, July 11:

They say therapy is good for the soul. And while Leo’s past activity has left some wondering if he even has a soul, his session with Marlena may uncover some very revealing information.

As Chad’s plan is put into motion, he and Jack make a discovery that will leave both of them shaken.

Get out your tissues, because Doug and Julie will share a loving moment today. And of course, given that his portrayer, Bill Hayes, passed away back in January, this is likely to be one of the last times we’ll see the couple together.

Usually, information coming to light about Everett/Bobby is shocking one of the locals. But today, an unexpected visitor’s arrival will leave the conflicted young man on the receiving end of the shock!

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Friday, July 12:

Perhaps because he lives in Salem, where all secrets eventually come to light, Stefan frets that what he’s been keeping under his hat will soon be exposed.

Rafe suggests that Gabi forget about what’s come before and instead focus on moving forward. But something tells us she’s not likely to listen…

Jada draws a line in the sand with Bobby/Everett. But will he/they take her ultimatum to heart?
