Rosabell Laurenti Sellers is an Italian-American actress.

She is the daughter of American actress Mary Sellers and Italian horror film director and screenwriter Fabrizio Laurenti. Her father was born in Rome, Lazio, Italy. Her mother was born in Nairobi, Kenya. She grew up in New York City and Rome. Rosabell founded Young Actors for Humanitarian Involvement, encouraging Italian teenage stars to participate in charities ActionAid and Smile Again.
