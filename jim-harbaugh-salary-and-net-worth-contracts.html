Biography & Education

On December 23, 1963, James Joseph Harbaugh was born in Toledo, Ohio.

Jim was raised alongside his brother John, who would go on to have a good football career, and his family traveled around a lot while his father took assistant coaching jobs at various institutions across the country.

During this time, he resided in Kentucky, Iowa, Michigan, Stanford, and other places.

Due to his family’s continual relocation, Jim attended numerous high schools until finally earning his California high school diploma in 1982.
