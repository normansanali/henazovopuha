Vinny Guadagnino Biography

Vinny Guadagnino is an American Reality Television Personality and Actor, who was born and brought up in Staten Island, New York. He is widely known for his appearances and roles in the MTV reality television series ‘Jersey Shore’ and its sequels which include ‘Jersey Shore Shark Attack’, and ‘Jersey Shore: Family Vacation’.

Vinny is also known for his appearances on notable films and series including the MTV hybrid talk show reality series ‘The Show with Vinny’, the reality TV series ‘Revenge Prank’ and in the MTV’s scripted comedy series ‘The Hard Times of RJ Berger’. His other notable roles include Hammerhead in ‘The Masked Dancer’, Alex in ‘Above Average Presents’, and Poker-Playing Celebrity in ‘90210’ TV Series.
