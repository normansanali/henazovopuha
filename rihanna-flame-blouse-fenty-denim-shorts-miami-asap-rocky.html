See Rihanna and A$AP Rocky's Matching Flame Outfits

Rihanna and A$AP Rocky looked flaming hot together in Miami.

Last night, the pair were spotted hand in hand on a date wearing coordinating neon-embellished ensembles that seemed to nod at a common fire theme. Rihanna wore a flame-printed Vetements top paired with mini Fenty denim shorts, along with a jumbo Pisces medallion by Briony Raymond. Rocky complemented her in a black tee featuring a bold, neon-green lightning bolt. He also wore a snapback with a matching neon-green visor and a black sweatshirt featuring the same lightning bolt symbol in orange.

BACKGRID

Rihanna has sported several other bold and bright fits alongside Rocky. She and the rapper were spotted out together in New York City in similar coordinated neon ensembles—Rihanna in a bright pink fur coat and Rocky in a complementary bright-green fur hat—while filming a music video atop a fire escape ahead of Rocky's upcoming album, All Smiles.

On a separate date night back in June, Rihanna was seen in a vintage John Galliano for Dior slipdress with a matching neon-pink bucket hat and pink nails. Days later, she reappeared in the city solo wearing a sultry lime-green polo shirt and black sequin-embellished skirt by Supriya Lele from the brand's respective fall-winter and spring-summer '21 collections.

Following years of speculation surrounding their romance, Rihanna and Rocky officially debuted as a serious item only months ago, with Rocky disclosing to GQ that the singer is the "love of [his] life" and also "the One." The rapper added in the interview that she's also "absolutely" been a source of inspiration behind his music and has assisted him in his creative process. "I think it's important to have somebody that you can bounce those creative juices and ideas off of," he shared. "It's just a different point of view."

In a separate interview, Rocky told Entertainment Tonight, "[It's] important having that support in real life. … It's amazing and I'm honestly truly blessed, for real."
