Biography

Silk Smitha was born in India on December 2, 1960. Remembered for her roles in numerous Malayalam, Tamil, Hindi, and Telugu films, this Indian actress is particularly notable for her portrayal of Silk in the 1979 movie Vandichakkaram. After appearing in this film, the actress previously known as Vijayalakshmi adopted the stage name of Silk Smitha. She co-starred with Kamal Haasan in the acclaimed 1982 Tamil film Moondram Pirai.
