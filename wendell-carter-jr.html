Wendell Carter Jr.

Wendell Carter Jr. is an American professional basketball player who plays for the NBA team, Chicago Bulls. He played college basketball at Duke.

Wendell’s Bio & Family

The 21-years-of-age athlete was born as Wendell Andre Carter Jr. on April 16, 1999, in Atlanta, Georgia. He is the son of a former professional basketball player father, Wendell Carter Sr. Besides, Wendell’s mother, Kylia Carter, played basketball at the University of Mississippi.

Wendell Carter Jr. wishes Mother’s Day to his mom with the throwback family picture on 10 May 2020 (Wendell’s Instagram)

Wendell attended Pace Academy for his high school education, where he began his amateur basketball career. Later, he transferred to Duke University and played college basketball for the Duke Blue Devils. The player holds African-American ethnicity and his birth sign is Aries.

Career & Net Worth

Wendell Carter Jr. made his way into the professional NBA league after being picked by the Chicago Bulls on June 21, 2018. He immediately signed the three-year contract with the Bulls worth $15,091,440 on July 3, 2018.

Wendell Carter Jr. signed with Chicago Bulls of NBA in 2018 (Wendell’s Instagram)

Eventually, the young player made his NBA debut scoring eight points and three assists against the Philadelphia 76ers on 18 October 2018. Later on March 24, 2019, he was ruled out of the remainder of the season with a left thumb surgery.

Wendell stands tall at a height of 6 feet and 9 inches (2.06 meters). Besides, he has a body weight of 270 pounds (122 kg). Moreover, the player has black hair and black eyes.
