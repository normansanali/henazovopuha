Where is Josh Rosen playing in 2024?

Josh Rosen, the talented quarterback, has been a subject of great interest in the football world. Speculations about his next team have been circulating, and there are several potential landing spots for him in 2024.

NFL: Potential Teams for Josh Rosen

New York Giants

The New York Giants have been identified as a potential landing spot for Rosen.

The Giants have been evaluating Rosen, and there were concerns within the organization about how he would handle the press when things went wrong on the field.

But reports indicate that the Giants are hesitant to give up too much for Rosen, and they are not inclined to trade either of their first-round picks for him.

There's uncertainty surrounding the Giants' plan at quarterback, making them a team to watch during the draft.

Arizona Cardinals

The Arizona Cardinals, Rosen's current team, might have an extra young quarterback available if they choose to draft Kyler Murray as the first overall pick in the draft, making Rosen expendable.

But the exact plans of the Cardinals are still unclear, and there have been conflicting reports about whether the Cardinals have seriously entertained a Rosen trade with any other team.

This uncertainty leaves Rosen's future with the Cardinals in question.

Other Potential NFL Teams

Apart from the Giants and the Cardinals, there are other NFL teams that could potentially be interested in acquiring Rosen. But the market for Rosen, as well as the seriousness of other teams in acquiring him, remains unclear at this point.

Potential Leagues and Teams Outside the NFL

Canadian Football League (CFL)

As an alternative to the NFL, the Canadian Football League (CFL) could be a potential destination for Rosen.

The CFL offers a different style of play and could provide Rosen with an opportunity to showcase his skills in a new environment. But it's important to note that the CFL has its own set of challenges and adjustments for players transitioning from the NFL.

Free Agency

With his talent and potential, Rosen could also explore possibilities in free agency. This could open up opportunities with various teams looking to strengthen their quarterback position.

Free agency allows players to negotiate with multiple teams and choose the best fit for their skills and career trajectory.

 

As of now, the future of Josh Rosen in 2024 remains uncertain. With the NFL draft approaching, the potential for a trade involving Rosen is still up in the air.

The New York Giants and Arizona Cardinals are among the teams that could influence Rosen's future.

Also, the possibility of exploring opportunities in the CFL or through free agency adds further intrigue to Rosen's potential destinations in 2024.

The football world eagerly awaits to see where Josh Rosen will be playing in 2024, and his journey could take him to the NFL, the CFL, or into free agency.
