The Shahs Of Sunset Star's Net Worth Revealed

Celebrity Net Worth states that Golnesa Gharachedaghi is believed to be worth $12 million. Per The Things, not only does GG have the highest net worth among the "Shahs of Sunset" cast, but she's also "one of the richest celebrities on the Bravo network," period. Reportedly, she rakes in a cool $100,000 per episode of the popular show. Now that she's a successful businesswoman in her own right, she doesn't need the $7,000 allowance her dad used to give her, per Distractify. 

She's definitely come a long way from being unemployed and living off an allowance. Now, she makes her own money, running her businesses GG Extensions and Wusah. And she takes care of her young son, Elijah, to boot (via ET), which, per People, was what she really wanted her entire life. Fame and fortune are nice, but for GG, motherhood is priceless.
