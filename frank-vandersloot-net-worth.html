Frank L. VanderSloot Wiki Biography

Born in Billings, Montana USA on August 14, 1948, Frank L. VanderSloot is an entrepreneur and businessman, renowned as the founder and CEO of Melaleuca, Inc., a health and wellness company. He previously served as the Vice-President of Cox Communications and also held positions on the board of directors and executive board of the US Chamber of Commerce, commencing his active career in the mid-1970s.

By late 2016, Frank VanderSloot’s net worth was estimated at $1.2 billion, amassed through his prosperous business endeavors.
