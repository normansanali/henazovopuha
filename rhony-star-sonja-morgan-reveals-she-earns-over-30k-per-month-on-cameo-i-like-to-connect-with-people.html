RHONY star Sonja Morgan reveals she earns over $30K PER MONTH on Cameo: 'I like to connect with peop

Sonja Morgan revealed the shocking chunk of change she earns every month on Cameo alone.

On Thursday, the Real Housewives of New York City vet, 60, admitted that she made over $30,000 recently on the popular video messaging website and app as one of the platform’s biggest stars.

While promoting The Real Housewives: Ultimate Girls Trip: RHONY Legacy, she told Us Weekly: ‘I’ve got, like, $38 [thousand] right now.’

When asked how much she charges fans for custom videos, she said she has assigned a price of just under a hundred dollars.

The reality TV personality — who recently confessed she is ‘hooked’ on her new beau despite only seeing him two days a week — added: ‘I do $99 ’cause … I can do a lot more.’

‘I like to connect with people,’ she said before noting that she is getting a lot of requests with the holidays approaching.

She told the outlet that around this time of year, a lot of people are looking for motivation and ‘a pep talk’ from her.

‘People are so stressed right now with the holidays and kids,’ she explained. 

‘And people get sick around the holidays, as you know, so I’m getting a lot of people needing a pep talk,’ she continued, noting that the winter holidays can be ‘hard for a lot of people’.

On the same day, her co-star, Kristen Taekman, 46, confirmed Morgan’s high earnings and told the outlet that it has become ‘a full-time job for her’.

However, Morgan said she was not only high earner on the app and noted that all of the franchise stars ‘do really well’ on Cameo.

‘You never get less than $35 thousand a month,’ she said of herself and the other Bravolebrities.

Dorinda Medley, 59, then shared her own experience and said she gets Cameo requests for motivation and inspiration just like Morgan, but she doesn’t make quite as much.
