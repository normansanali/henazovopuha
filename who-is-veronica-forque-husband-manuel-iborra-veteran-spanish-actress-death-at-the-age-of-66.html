Who is Veronica Forque Husband Manuel Iborra? Veteran Spanish Actress Death At The Age of 66

Veronica Forque Suicidio Death At Age 66

She got to witness more than 6 decades of beautiful life but unfortunately, she had to leave the world in such a horrific way which is a sad thing to know.
