Who is Sophia Perez from Temptation Island?

USA Networks reality series Temptation Island kicked off it's third season in February 2021.

The show features several couples who agree to live with a group of singles of the opposite sex, in order to test the strength of their relationships.

Who is Sophia Perez from Temptation Island?

Miami native Sophia Perez, 23, is a cast member in season three of Temptation Island.

The 23-year-old makeup artist is described as "the life of the party" on her USA Network bio.

"A Cuban American living in Miami, FL, Sophia is the life of the party.

"After a very recent breakup, she’s excited to experience the single life, ready to have some fun and hopes to move on to a better relationship while on Temptation Island."

According to the bio, Perez owns Glamorous Looks Studio, a Miami-based hair and makeup team and also specializes in makeup education.

Perez has over 40,000 followers on her Instagram page.

How many couple are on this season of Temptation Island?

There are four couples and 23 singles in this season of Temptation Island.

The couples include:

  • Chelsea and Thomas: the West Hollywood couple have been together for a year
  • Erica and Kendal: this Los Angeles couple have been in a relationship for two years
