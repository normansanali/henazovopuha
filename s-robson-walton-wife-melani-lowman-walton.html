S. Robson Walton wife: Melani Lowman-Walton

Melani A. Lowman Walton was born in July 1974 and grew up in Montana. She is currently 48years old and became the third wife of Rob Walton, the chairman of Walmart when they got married in 2005.

Not much is known about Melanie’s childhood but it is on record that she graduated in 1998 from Dickinson State University in Dickinson, North Dakota with a Bachelor of Science in Art Education.

S. Robson Walton wife: Melani Lowman-Walton

Melani is a former athletic trainer and basketball coach. Furthermore, she has worked in commercial real estate.

Melani lives in Paradise Valley, Arizona with her husband S Rob Walton of the famous Walton Family. They both share a passion for owning and driving vintage race cars.

Melani A. Lowman Walton is into philanthropy. She has since September 2010, made political contributions of about $14,900 to Republican politicians.

She is co-founder of Arizona State University’s “Sustainability Solutions Service” The service provides continuing education for teachers and science educators and educates the public about global environmental problems and solutions.

She and her husband have also been active in community outreaches and projects as Melani Lowman manages the Walton Family Foundation.

Also, Melani serves as a trustee of the Theodore Roosevelt Presidential Library Foundation, which is slated for 2025.
