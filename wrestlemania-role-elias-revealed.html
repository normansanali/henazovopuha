WrestleMania Role for Elias Revealed

While there were plans for Elias to have a non-wrestling segment at Wrestlemania 34 this year, it now appears that those plans are completely off the table.

WWE has updated the list of Andre The Giant Memorial Battle Royal participants and Elias has been added to the list. Unless he is the first participant in the match, it is very unlikely that we will get to ‘Walk with Elias’ on Sunday night.
